Knowledge is power.
 
Take the anxiety out of charging your vehicle with the help of Intelligent Range.* This feature lets you know how much energy your F-150® Lightning™ uses while factoring in things like weather, traffic, elevation, towing, payload and more. So you have the trust and peace of mind that you have the power you need to get the job done.

You’re in charge.
 
Ford offers three different options for charging your F-150® Lightning™ pickup at home. Like all Ford BEVs, F-150 Lightning comes with a 32-amp Ford Mobile Charger, which allows you to charge your truck from a traditional 120V or 240V outlet. Looking for a faster charge? There’s an available Ford Connected Charge Station for that. And with an extended-range battery, you’ll get the Ford Charge Station Pro for a full charge overnight (10 hours).* Choose exactly the level of power you need, when you need it.

Relax, we’ve got it mapped out.
 
Whether you’re traveling cross-country or running a quick errand, stay on track with FordPass.™* The app helps you plan trips using your device or SYNC® screen to check your charge level, find charging locations on your trip (from over 16,000 stations) and map your favorite routes while taking things like payload, towing and charging costs into consideration. So you can spend less time planning and more time doing.

The benefits of electric are adding up.
 
Not only is the new F-150® Lightning™ a capable and productive vehicle, it’ll help save you money. You could save as much as $7,500* in federal tax incentives depending on where you live. Plus, the scheduled maintenance costs of F-150 Lightning are estimated to be 40% less than those of a gas-powered F-150.** Just a few perks of owning the MVP of BEVs.
